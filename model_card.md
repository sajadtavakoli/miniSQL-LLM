# Model Card: miniSQL-LLM

## Purpose
Educational text-to-SQL assistant fine-tuned for converting database schemas and natural-language questions into SQL.

## Intended use
Research, learning, and portfolio demonstration.

## Limitations
Generated SQL may be incorrect or unsafe. Review before running against real databases.

## Training data
The initial repository includes a small synthetic sample. Replace or extend it with Spider, WikiSQL, or your own curated examples.
